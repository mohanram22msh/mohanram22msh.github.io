TS Model School Inter Admission 2023

TSMS invites online applications from eligible TS SSC students for admission to various courses of Telangana Model Schools for the academic year 2023-2024. An online application released on TS Model School Admission web portal for admission in Telangana State Model Schools.

Applications are invited for admission to various Intermediate courses to TS Model Schools. Candidates will be selected on the basis of merit. TS Model College has 40 seats in each stream – MPC, BIPC, CEC and MEC. To apply online, students can apply by visiting tsmodelschools.com. The process to apply online is shared in this post.

Important Dates for TSMS

Model School Inter Admission 2023 Eligibility Criteria

  • Applicants must have Passed their 10th Class From Any Recognised Board.
  • Students must have All the Documents asked for During the Admission Form Submission.
  • Candidate has to Pass TS Model School Entrance Exam 2023.

Documents Required For TS Model Admission 2023

  • Aadhar Card
  • 10th Marksheet
  • Domicile Certificate
  • Date of Birth
  • Entrance Exam Score Card
  • Residential Proof

How to Apply for TS Model School Inter Admission 2023

  • Follow the steps given below to apply –
  • First of all eligible students have to visit the official website tsmodelschools.com.
  • You can apply online by clicking on the Admission link in the home page of the official website.
  • Finally, by clicking on the submit button, candidates can take a print out of the application form.
  • Note: Download the hard copy of the filled application and affix your passport photo and hand it over to the principal where he/she wants to take admission.
